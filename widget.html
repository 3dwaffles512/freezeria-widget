Imagine you are working at an ice cream shop. Each sundae has a certain number of scoops.

In coding, a variable is like a small box that remembers a value for us. In this activity, our variable is called scoops, and it remembers how many scoops of ice cream are in the cup.

Play with the scoops variable

Use the buttons to change the scoops variable. Watch how the number and the ice cream scoops in the cup change.
